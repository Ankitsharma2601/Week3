ABOUT US

Location & Accessibility

The institute is located on the Tata-Kandra main road at a distance of about 8 km and 3 km from Tata Nagar Railway Station and Adityapur Railway Station respectively. It is situated in the industrial hub of Adityapur. The major industries that are benefiting the students include Tata Steel, Tata Motors, Tayo Rolls Ltd., Usha Martin Ltd., Adhunik Steel and many more. N.I.T. Jamshedpur is at a distance of about 2 km towards South. The simplest and the most economical way to reach is by bus or auto-rickshaw which runs throughout the day.

Examination

The examination and certification of the selected and admitted candidates are done by State Board of Technical Education (SBTE), Ranchi. At present, semester system. Minimum 75% attendance of the students is necessary for appearing at their Board Examinations conducted by State Board of Technical Education(SBTE), Ranchi, Jharkhand.
